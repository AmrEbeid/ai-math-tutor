What is 48 divided by 6?
Good one — let's work it out together. Division is sharing into equal groups. If 48 cookies are shared by 6 friends, how many each? Start with what you know: what's 6 × 8?
6 × 8 is 48!
Exactly 👏 So if 6 groups of 8 make 48, then 48 ÷ 6 = 8. You found it yourself.

How it teaches

One step at a time — with a question back

Asks

Your child: "I don't get fractions." Zeluu doesn't lecture — it asks what they already know and starts there.

Hints

Instead of the answer, it offers a hint and a small, doable next step — then waits for your child to try.

Checks

It checks understanding before moving on, and only reveals more once your child is ready. The answer is earned, not handed over.
